Coffee Shop App
Welcome to the Coffee Shop App! This application is designed to enhance your experience in finding and purchasing your favorite coffee and coffee beans. Whether you are looking for a quick espresso or browsing for the perfect blend of beans, our app makes it easy and convenient for you.

Features
User-Friendly Interface: Navigate through our intuitive design to find exactly what you need.
Product Search: Easily search for specific coffee products, including beverages and coffee beans.
Personalized Recommendations: Get recommendations based on your preferences and purchase history.
Secure Payments: Safe and secure payment options for a hassle-free checkout experience.
Order Tracking: Stay updated with the status of your order from preparation to delivery.
Customer Reviews: Read and write reviews for different products to share your experiences.
Loyalty Program: Earn points with every purchase and redeem them for discounts and exclusive offers.
